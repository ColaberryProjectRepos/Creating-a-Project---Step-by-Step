Submitting for 2nd Level Approval
********************************************************************


Once you are  finished with the questions, click on the back button to head back to the projects.



Find the project in the Level 1 approved section and click on the "Final Submit" button.  This button is also at the very bottom of the disputes page  but are not activated/shown unless

"All Disputes have been fixed"





There are 2 approval processes for this so with any of the 2 approves, you may receive feedback and asked to make changes on your project.  Once you make the changes, you will submit again.
You will keep repeating this process until you get through both approvals.  Once you make it through the 2nd approval process, that's where you will find out if your project will be a...


Public Project:  A project that can be seen by the Public.  You have the ability to share these projects.

Private Project: A project that can only be seen by Colaberry Network.  You do NOT have the ability to share these projects.


You should always strive to create work good enough to be Public.  To see public projects, log out of app.colaberry.com and then access the Colaberry Network page to see what people without access see.


Even after you submit the Project for approval, continue to make more changes as disputes come in.  This is a built to be a continuous process even after submitting for approval.


While awaiting project approval, start working on your deployment.  Once you have made all of your changes to the project, redeploying is much more simple than the initial deployment setup.


Colaberry Staff has reporting that inforces that these projects don't go more than 2 days without 2nd level project approval.  If that is not the case, reach out to your Tier II  Manager.  If you don't get a response from them, reach out to the program Director @ShemikaHopson.  If she doesn't respond, then tag @Ali.  Either way, be very descriptive in explaining what the problem is.
