Daily Updates:
Every day you work on your project, you will comment on your ticket with 

  • Which step you are on in the Documentation Process
  • A detailed summary of what you worked on 
  • Screenshot of what you worked on 


Managing Due Dates:

Due dates are initially setup based on the time length you picked to complete the project

  • All task must have due dates at all time.  Do NOT clear the due dates
  • All due dates are in the future. If you have a due date in the past, it is late and will reflect so on your Project Dashboard.
  • Before you change any of the dates, you must add a comment to your project answering these 4 questions.

                                a.  Reason for changing date:

                                      b.  Screenshot of your Project Dashboard

                                      c.  New Due Date

                                      d.  Why you think you will complete the project by the new date


          If your project enters the late status, you will start receiving emails and approached by your Project Delivery Manager Kofi.  The Project Manager will start questioning you on your progress.

          Here is an example of documenting your tasks.

          Example:




          Instructions:

          1. Assign this task to yourself.
          2. Once you have read through this task and you fully understand it, write a small paragraph with screenshots and notes of what you learned.  Then add it as a comment on this task.
          3. Everyday you work on this task, you will add a minimal of a comment and a screenshot.  Gifs are good as well.
          4. Note: You as well as other people will be reading through your project notes.  Make sure you talk about your thoughts at the moment so you and others can relive the experience.
          5. Upon completion, close out the task by marking it complete and navigate back to your Colaberry Project Dashboard.  The Dashboard will be updated hourly.
