How to get help with your Project 6

********************************************************************
Support Process
********************************************************************


As you work on your project, you may need guidance or insight.  The purpose of the Center of Excellence is to use the minds of many to make each of us better.

  
 
1st Level: 
Colaberry Network  
 
2nd Level:
For more extensive interactions such as Dashboard Reviews, you will use Message Board.


You will categorize it as a question.  It is ok to ask others to critique your work.  People will give feedback that will help you improve.  Also Colaberry staff will participate.


3rd Level: 

If you are unable to get help through the following 2 sources

 

Then you can tag your <<Tier II Leader>>

(<<Tier II Leader>>  Names can be found in Project Overview task.)


Please make sure you use the 1st 2 levels first as the goal is to increase activity in our Center of Excellence and you will probably get a faster response there. Type the @ and then start typing the name.  You can ask the question directly in your ticket.  Be very specific with your question and include screenshots as always.


Instructions:

  1. Assign this task to yourself.
  2. Once you have read through this task and you fully understand it, write a small paragraph with screenshots and notes of what you learned.  Then add it as a comment on this task.
  3. Everyday you work on this task, you will add a minimal of a comment and a screenshot.  Gifs are good as well.
  4. Note: You as well as other people will be reading through your project notes.  Make sure you talk about your thoughts at the moment so you and others can relive the experience.
  5. Upon completion, close out the task by marking it complete and navigate back to your Colaberry Project Dashboard.  The Dashboard will be updated hourly.




Note: This is a new process and if something doesn't seem complete, please go check the step in the original project instructions because that is the 100% source of truth at the moment.  I will remove this note once I've confirmed the steps will always match 100%.
