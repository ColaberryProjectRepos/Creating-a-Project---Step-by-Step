    ********************************************************************

    Designing a data science project involves careful planning and consideration of various factors to ensure its success. Here are the key steps to follow when designing a data science project:

    1. Identify the Problem or Objective:
      • Clearly define the problem or objective that the data science project aims to address. Ensure that the problem is well-understood and has clear goals and deliverables.
    2. Understand the Business Context:
      • Gain a deep understanding of the business context surrounding the problem. This includes understanding the industry, the organization's goals, and how the project aligns with broader business objectives.
    3. Define Key Stakeholders:
      • Identify the key stakeholders involved in the project, including domain experts, decision-makers, and end-users. Establish communication channels to ensure their input and feedback are incorporated.
    4. Data Acquisition and Sources:
      • Determine where and how you will obtain the necessary data for the project. Identify data sources, data formats, and any data collection or integration requirements.
