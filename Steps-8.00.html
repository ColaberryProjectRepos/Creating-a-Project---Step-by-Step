Dataset and Industry KPI's
********************************************************************



Industry KPI's are chosen by industry professionals that have been in that Industry most their life.  When picking a dataset, we must understand which Industry our data is in and make sure we find KPI's which are recognized in the Industry.  This way you can relate to the clients that will be viewing your project.


The following project will walk you through that process.  You will learn about how to search for Datasets and Industry KPI's.  By the time you move on, you should have a dataset and  2-3 Industry KPI's per Dashboard.  For Data Warehousing projects, you can use KPI's that monitor your package.


Towards the end of the below project, you will be asked to Stress test your chosen dataset and KPI's to ensure you don't waste your times with a bad dataset/KPI's.  After getting your Dataset and KPI's approved, you will continue with these next steps.   


Never stop moving forward.  You are to continue to multitask and let nothing stop your progress.  The stress test may take a couple of days but that is no reason why you can't start exploring your data.  The stress test will produce either validation or cause you to alter your design, but either way it will save you tons of time in the grand scheme of things.
