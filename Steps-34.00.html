Completing the Project
********************************************************************


Currently your project is somewhere between Level 2 and 3 approval.  You must still work deligently to fix the issues and get your project approved.  That is your priority, but you should not be sitting around waiting for disputes to come in.  You must keep moving forward while fixing disputes as they come in.


Complete the Section:

Navigate back to your curriculum and now you will have all the information you need for your Lab Test and Survey.


After Completing the Section:

  • JRP - If you are in JRP, after completing your Project in the curriculum, you will head back to the IPBC Dashboard / JRP page.  There are more task for you there before you move on to your next project.  There will be a task in your current JRP Phase where you will have to show your approved project on Colaberry Network so you have more time to get the disputes fixed and changes in, but there are also other task for you to complete so you are not stagnate. 


  • Alumni - If you are on Project 1-4 then move on to your next project. If this is your 5th project, you will be assigned a badge for your tool upon completion of this section and approval of your last project.  Congratulations!
