What to focus on while working through your Project
********************************************************************


Download Project Checklist:

Click on the Red Button at the top of the question to download the Project Checklist.  This checklist will be the guide you will fill out as you are working through your project.


Project requirements  - Your project requirements are stored in the curriculum.  They should also be located in the ticket you created for your project.  You should always keep them close as they are the guide you need to building your project.


Identitfy your KPIs - The goal here is to find your data set along with Industry specific KPI's.  It is more of a research process and the better you do here, the easier it will be to build your project.


Visualize your Dataset - Create a visual of your Dataset.  As you are coming up with idea, it easier to brainstorm when you have a good picture of the dataset.


Visualize Key Insights - This task helps you with your Data Analyst skills.  You will be taught how to Identify trending and detect anamolies so it can be documented in your project.


Troubles that occured on the project - There will be complications.  Document these because it will be great insight for others that build your project after you.


Problem Statement - The whole reason for creating your project will be outlined in the Problem Statement.  Of course you don't know that right this minute, but once you complete the above steps, it will make it easier to come up with one after the project is built.



Envisioning the Next Few Steps:


The next few steps will walk your through your project checklist.    Once you finish your project and have just about completed your Project Checklist, you will Stress Test your project by having others in the C.O.E. critique it.  After using that process to wrap up your project, you will then take screenshots of the project, add them to your checklist and then get ready to submit your Project for Level 1 approval.
