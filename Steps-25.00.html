Visual Suggestions

  • This section will be in two parts - Text & Pictures
  • Now that you know the data, based on your insight, what are some of changes you would suggest to stop some of the problems you see.  1st identify a problem  in the data and then come up with a solution to that problem.
  • In the text portion, you will give 2-3 problems  solutions. 
  • Each step should be numbered
  • In the Pictures section you will find a picture that represents each solution.
  • In the pictures, you could take screenshots of actual parts of your project or you could show pictures that relate to your problem.  Remember to always capture the hearts of the audience.
  • Numbers must be numbered with same font / size / text / color to keep consistency
  • There should be no titles except before the numbered steps


Note: This is a fact based study.  Make your suggestions more geared towards the data.  Put a fact in front of your opinion and it adds validity to your advice.

Examples:


Suggestions:                                                                           

1.     In 2020, unemployment rate increased to 8.32% which is a 124% jump compared to the previous year 3.7%. In this case, the government should step in and apply different monetary and fiscal policy measures.
2.     The data trend shows a huge gap among racial groups; especially Black or African Americans are highly underprivileged in all labor force matrices. Fight for equal opportunity to all races and promote gender equality in the job market. Funding education is also an effective long term solution. Better-educated people can get jobs easily.
3.     Raising the incentive for companies to employ people and open branches in low performing regions like Nevada and West Virginia. This can often be accomplished by giving firms financial incentives like tax advantages or other incentives to offer jobs for the local population.
