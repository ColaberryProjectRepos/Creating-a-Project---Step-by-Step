Understanding Project Disputes
********************************************************************


At this point, you should see your project start to evolve.  The 2 previous stress test should have helped with the design and messaging.  Now it is time to refine your step by step instructions by having others create disputes for you to fix.  There are 2 Types of Disputes.


Peer Disputes: In the following step you will Stress Test your step by step instructions by posting a link of your project to the C.O.E. and having them critique it by adding disputes of your project in app.colaberry.com.


Admin Disputes: Once you submit your project, it will now be critiqued by a Colaberry Admin.  The admin will create disputes as well.


You can see how many disputes you have when accessing your Level 1 approved project.




Fixing Disputes:

You will receive emails when a new set of disputes  come in.  To access them, you can click on the link in the email or you can click on the Project Instructions button located on your Level 1 approved project.  You will scroll down to the bottom of the project to see the disputes section.


Viewing Disputes:

Once you expand on the dispute you will see the screenshot and explanation of why the dispute was created.  Once you make the adjustment, you will click on the "Log Fix"




Here you will fill out the below screen.

Explanation: What did you fix.

Change Screenshot: Screenshot of the fix.


Admin Disputes will have to be validated after your fix.

Peer Disputes will immidiately be validated after the fix.


Red - New Dispute

Yellow / Orange - Fixed & Non Validated Dispute

Green - Fixed & Validated Dispute


Each dispute you fix makes your project a little bit better.  The next step will teach you how to stress test your Project Instructions.  Once you set up the stress test, disputes will start coming in.  You will not see admin disputes until you submit your project for Level 2 Approval.



Instructions:

  1. Assign this task to yourself.
  2. Once you have read through this task and you fully understand it, write a small paragraph with screenshots and notes of what you learned.  Then add it as a comment on this task.
  3. Everyday you work on this task, you will add a minimal of a comment and a screenshot.  Gifs are good as well.
  4. Note: You as well as other people will be reading through your project notes.  Make sure you talk about your thoughts at the moment so you and others can relive the experience.
  5. Upon completion, close the task and navigate back to your Colaberry Project Dashboard.


Note: This is a new process and if something doesn't seem complete, please go check the step in the original project instructions because that is the 100% source of truth at the moment.  I will remove this note once I've confirmed the steps will always match 100%.
