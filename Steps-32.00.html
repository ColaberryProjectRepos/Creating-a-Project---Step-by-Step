Once you have completed the Deployment process, come back here and finish out the instructions.


Last Step

Once you have deployed to the Colaberry Server, you will add one last step to your Project which will be called "Deployment".


You will need get the html link which will allow you to deploy your solution to a website.  It will look similar to below as it will start and end with iframe




In the step insertion, you will click on the html tag button and paste the HTML code.


Then click on the html code button AGAIN and you will see the deployed project embedded into the step.  Scroll to the top and be sure to include  the username and pw if not able to connect from public location just like below.



If your deployment is a Dashboard, be sure to leave a little note at the top letting your users know they can now interact with your Dashboard.  Your project is now fully deployed and accessible to all users in the Colaberry Network!!!
