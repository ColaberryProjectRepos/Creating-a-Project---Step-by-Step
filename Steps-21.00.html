Visual Problem Statement
********************************************************************


A problem is a statement about an area of concern, a condition to be improved upon, a difficulty to be eliminated, or a troubling question that exists in theory or in practice that points to the need for meaningful understanding and deliberate investigation.


Structure of a Problem Statement


FYI: If you followed the Project Documentation while you were building your project, it will help you gather the information needed to help with this process.   Now that you understand what  a Problem statement is by watching the video above, after completing it you  will take time to create a Visual depiction of your Problem Statement.  Be creative.  People don't like to read.  You have to draw people into your work.  You want to make it enjoyable for them so they will share it.  


Visual Problem Statement Examples


  • Your project statement will be in two parts - Text & Pictures
  • Problem statement should only be 3-5 sentences
  • It will include at least one stat that you found on the internet which helps describe how significant the problem is.
  • The above stat will be colored red or green based on if the number is good or bad.
  • The problem statement will be in paragraph form with the PowerPoint slide with pictures below it
  • You want to capture the hearts of the audience so use pictures that really show the human side of your story.


Remember...

The problems statement will be the reason for the entire solution you are building.  You will find something on the internet that talks about your data and something related to your KPI's. Draw the user in with a dramatic picture.  You want to make the problem seem like the worst problem because people love drama and it will attract them to your project more.  Also the worse the problem is, the better your solution seems.  If you can't find a problem from the outside, use your dashboard to find the worst stat.  Then put it in your problem statement, but don't make it look like that part came from your data.



Examples:


Problem Statement
Flight operations is challenging on many different levels, largely because of the different agencies and people involved, not to mention the physical and environmental aspects.  According to the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, in 2019 alone, 21% of the total flights in the US were delayed.  The reality is that as long as airplanes continue flying, flight delays will always be a part of the experience.
