Every project will be documented.  Not only will the document the project at completion, but you will also document the things you worked on everyday you work on it. 


When you finish working on a project and a couple of weeks go by, you will forget all the details of everything you worked on.  That's why it is extremely important to use this process while working throughout your project. 


Navigate to your Center of Excellence program based on your Tier II Tool.




By now you should have already created a Project through our automated process.  If you have not, navigate here and click on "Add Project" to get started.  That link also takes you to Project Dashboard which will give you insight on how well you are doing on your project.


Our automated process allows you to answer a few questions like the name of the project and how soon do you want to finish it.  It then goes out to Basecamp (ProjectMgmt) and builds all the tasks and stress test for you.  Then all you have to do is manage your task just as you would in the real world.  This is not close to the real thing, this IS the real thing.  Enjoy the experience.


Instructions:

  1. Assign this task to yourself.
  2. Once you have read through this task and you fully understand it, write a small paragraph with screenshots and notes of what you learned.  Then add it as a comment on this task.
  3. Everyday you work on this task, you will add a minimal of a comment and a screenshot.  Gifs are good as well.
  4. Note: You as well as other people will be reading through your project notes.  Make sure you talk about your thoughts at the moment so you and others can relive the experience.
  5. Upon completion, close out the task by marking it complete and navigate back to your Colaberry Project Dashboard.  The Dashboard will be updated hourly.
