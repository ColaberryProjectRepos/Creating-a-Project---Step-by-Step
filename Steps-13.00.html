******Accelerated Learning******

While going through this critique process, we will accelerate your learning by using the projects that have been critiqued previously to shorten your learning time.  Follow these steps to enhance your speed of learning while going through this stress test process.


  • Go to your Basecamp Center of Excellence Message Board and search for "approved" projects . Read completely through 5 different critiqueed projects (including the comments) and for each project you go through, take notes of the different mistakes and take screenshots of cool things you see that might fit in your dashboard.  Take notes and screenshots on all 5 approved projects.
  • Use your notes to make your Project better.
  • When you submit for the Stress Test, your critiquer will respond to your crtiques 1-2 days after you submit it.  It is very important that you are not sitting around waiting for the critiques.  Take this time to go through more approved projects in between critiques.  Doing this will speed up your process because you will learn more and faster through past critiques than waiting for a live person to give you a crtique.  Most mistakes have already been made.  It's up to you to be resourceful and use the data to accelerate your learning.
  • Once you get close to having your project approved, you can start using the time in between critiques to move on and start working on your messaging.


A foolish developer dives into a project right away without taking time to take a step back to plan the big picture. 


A wise developer understands that without good planning, more time will be spent completing the projects.


By going through the previous projects, you can learn 10+ lessons in 1 hr.  A critiquer will deliver those same results in 3-5 days.  If you want to make it through the project faster, spend more time looking through other critiques and projects before you.

There is one main critiquer for your project.  Their updates will generally come about 1-2 days after you submit the critique.  When you are early on in your Stress Test, take this time in between critiques to explore other projects.  Find a cool project on Colaberry Network and then do a search for it in Basecamp.  Then read through the entire project to see where it started, where it finished and how it got there.  It will increase your knowledge tremendously with each project you look through.  It will also give you ideas on how to improve your projects.


Once you get further into your critique, you can go ahead and start moving forward in the documentation process while you are fielding crtiques.  You should not be in a situation where you sit around waiting.  Throughout this Project Building process, you should always be moving forward and never delayed by the process.
