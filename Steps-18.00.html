Adding Project Tags
********************************************************************





Click on the "Project Tags" button to update the project tags.  You will see the screen below.




It is extremely important that you go through the tags and click all that apply.  The tags you choose will help Clients relate to your experience.


Category:  This is the highest level classification of your project.  Click all that apply.


Industry: Choose an Industry that is related to your dataset.


Tools: This category is for all the tools/software used on the project.  


Contributions:  If you are working with mulitple people on this project, you will want to check their name so the project shows up under their name as well.  This will also allow them to edit the Project Instructions.  Ensure they have filled out a Colaberry Project BIO form to see their name on the list.
