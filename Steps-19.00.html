Adding Project Instructions
********************************************************************




By clicking on the "Project Instructions" button, each question added here will go in your project Instructions.


Click onthe "Add Question" button to add a question/step




You will see the following screen




Step Name:  This is a 3-7 word summary of the step you are performing


DataFile: If you have a script or information the user must download before moving forward, you will upload it here as a zip file.  Uploaded files will show a red icon at the top of the step indicating something needs to be downloaded.


Detailed Instructions: Here is where you will post a detailed explanations and screnshots/gifs of the step instructions.


Insight: Always try to give a reference that will help give a more meaningful explantion to the request.  It could be a YouTube video or another Colaberry Network project that will give you more insight on the project.


You will follow this process when adding each step.  The more expalanatory the better chance the users will complete your project.  The goal is to be so descriptive that every person that attemps this project  completes it.  There will be Analyitcs to come with KPI's like Penetration Rate (How far a person makes it through your project before leaving) and Completion Rate (% of people that critique the project and finish it.)  Gifs are small videos you can create vs. screenshots.  It allows you to condense your documentation and make your project easier to follow.  Most of your Step by Step documentation should include gifs.


Exmple of filled out step.





Instructions:

  1. Assign this task to yourself.
  2. Once you have read through this task and you fully understand it, write a small paragraph with screenshots and notes of what you learned.  Then add it as a comment on this task.
  3. Everyday you work on this task, you will add a minimal of a comment and a screenshot.  Gifs are good as well.
  4. Note: You as well as other people will be reading through your project notes.  Make sure you talk about your thoughts at the moment so you and others can relive the experience.
  5. Upon completion, close the task and navigate back to your Colaberry Project Dashboard.


Note: This is a new process and if something doesn't seem complete, please go check the step in the original project instructions because that is the 100% source of truth at the moment.  I will remove this note once I've confirmed the steps will always match 100%.
