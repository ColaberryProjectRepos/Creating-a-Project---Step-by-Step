Adding the Steps

  1. When adding steps, the 1st 5-6 slides will be your messaging.  Simply take your approved messaging and add them as the 1st steps on your project.  Start with the Problems statement text and slide as step 1.  
  2. Right after the messaging steps, you will add your step by step instructions that were approved with Stress Test 2.  
  3. If your project has a deployment, it will be in the last step, but that will come at a later step.  For now, add the messaging and step by step instructions.



1.  Step Name - This is a very short phrase to describe the step.

2. Data File - This is where you will upload all of the files needed for this step.  Upload them in zip files ONLY.

3. Detailed Instructions - This is where you will add the detailed instructions for each step.  Use Gifs to condense instructions.  We don't want it to

4. Insight - These are loaded at the end of the question.  All Step by Step Instructions, you will provide insight for.  That means a video or article that further helps a struggling user.  This is just to give them a resource that will dive more into the topic of the step.  Maybe it's a download for the tool you are using.

Note: You should already have the majority of the information you need from Stress Test 2 & 3.  You will need to add insight to every step and mask your links (Don't show the long link but instead have small text describing the link, but opens in a another tab when clicked on) 


Here are some more rules to follow...
  • Be sure to upload any files needed in the Data File section.  They should be uploaded as a zip file ONLY.  If you have multiple files be sure to label them accordingly and put them all in the zip file before uploading
  • Number your steps within a Question so it is clear what to do.
  • Use GIFs as much as possible and Keep your GIF's under 4 mb
  • Keep your Steps subject based.  If they don't relate to the step name, create another step.  Do not create really long steps.  It tires the user out.  It's better for them to have to click to move to the next step then keep scrolling
  • We are tracking all of the clicks so we know where people struggle throughout your project.  We will show those stats later.

Use the power of numbers to learn from others.  Go view a few projects and take what you like about each one and create your own process.  FYI - The most clicked on projects in Colaberry Network show up 1st.
